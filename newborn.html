Your baby's first days are precious. Tiny fingers, sleepy cuddles, and the details that change far too quickly. Preserving the beginning of your baby's story is so special.

These sessions are perfect for capturing the beauty of this new beginning in a sinple, natural, and timeless way.

You can choose to havr your session in a rented studio space near you, or in the comfort of your home where you can relax and simply soak in these precious moments.

I focus on gentle, unposed storytelling-soft snuggles, sleepy yawns, and the deep connection between you and your baby. Whether it's wuiry moments with just you and yiur newborn or the joy of siblings and parents embracing their newest gift from God. Each image is a reflection in love and warmth.

These sessions are slow-paced and baby-led. This ensures a calm, stress-free experience for everyone.

It is best to schedule these sessions between 20-28 weeks to ensure you get on the schedule. The session will take place within the first month or two of your baby being born. Let's capture your new season of life and newest blessing.
